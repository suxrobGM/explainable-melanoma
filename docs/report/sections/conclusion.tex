\section{Conclusion}

We described MelanomaNet, a dermoscopy classifier whose predictions can be
inspected through attention maps, extracted ABCDE criteria, concept
sensitivities, and uncertainty estimates. On ISIC 2019 it attains 81.7\%
balanced accuracy and a macro-F1 of 0.80 while producing explanations that we
evaluate for alignment and faithfulness rather than only display. The system is
not a finished clinical tool, but it shows that an accurate lesion classifier
can also be made accountable to the clinical concepts and confidence judgements
that its users rely on.
